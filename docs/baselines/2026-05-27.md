## What changed between this baseline and the prior one

The pre-fix Codex batch (`batch-20260527T042349Z-e617`) had **19 ✓ · 11 ✗ · 4 ⊘ · 0 —**. The three barf fixes landed this round account for the swing:

- **`f4686e1`** — `CODEX_TOOL_MAP` alias (`spawn_agent → Agent`),
  `custom_tool_call/apply_patch` handling, and the
  `stage="qa-agent-misconfigured"` diagnostic for QA-agent cwd
  mis-launches. Recovered 5 false-fail SDD-flavored scenarios and 1
  indeterminate from silent confusion to a named diagnostic.
- **`637dae9`** — `barf/bin/worktree-created` semantic check
  replacing `tool-called EnterWorktree` on
  `worktree-creation-from-main`; `worktree-creation-under-pressure`
  gated to claude-only. Recovered 1 false-fail; correctly skipped 1.
- **`921812b`** — `codex-subagent-wait-mapping` scenario updated to
  use canonical `Agent` post-alias. Recovers 1 fail on next batch.

After this round, the remaining non-passes are **agent-behavior
signal** (the Pattern 1/7 brainstorming-vs-X cluster, the Pattern 2
worktree case, the Pattern 5 stuck-poll cluster), not
**barf-tooling noise**.
